Logging in to E-cop.

By registering with eCops you will receive regular messages from Cambridgeshire Constabulary, about the area in which you live and work.

You have full access to your account settings - logging in allows you to control all of your personal details and adjust the types of messages you. want to receive. All of this is done through our secure servers.


Registering with E-cops


Registering is free, simple and easy - follow the step by step instructions by clicking the 'register' button above. Once you have verified your account by responding to the verification email, you will be ready to receive messages from us, based on the location you have provided and the things you have said are of interest to you.



Data Protection


Personal data provided to Information Providers by users of this website will be treated in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998. Information will not be sold, loaned or disclosed to any other user unless required to by law.
br All information is stored in a secure environment, all user passwords are encrypted and the entire system is security checked each month. Please review the website terms before completing your registration.
